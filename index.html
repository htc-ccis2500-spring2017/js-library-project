Example of WheelNav.js library
  • This is my example of something that can be built through the use of the wheelnav.js library. Although this is a simple example there are literally no limits to what can be done with this library. Click on the biohazard icon to continue.
  • You could use this library to create a unique way to navigate a website or you could even create a form where each "slice" of the pie navigates to a different input portion of the form. Click on the globe.
  • Another example could be a music player where each slice controls the music in different ways, such as play, fast forward, etc. Apple is better than Android. :-)
  • The wheel below shows how a marker can indicate which item is selected while the above wheel turns into a cog and the icon color turns to white. The above wheel also has a "spreader" in the center that can be used to collapse and expand the wheel. Give it a try!
